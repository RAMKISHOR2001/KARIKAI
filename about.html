About ToteBag Co.

Our Story, Values, and Mission

Who We Are

Karikai is a college startup founded by five fine arts students from Mumbai in 2024. We started with a passion for sustainable design and handcrafted products, creating beautiful, hand-painted tote bags that combine artistic expression with functional design.

Every tote bag is meticulously hand-painted by our talented team of artists, ensuring each piece is unique and reflects our commitment to quality craftsmanship. We use eco-friendly materials and sustainable production methods, making each bag not just beautiful, but also kind to the environment.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to using eco-friendly materials and sustainable production methods that minimize our environmental footprint.

Quality

Every tote bag is crafted with attention to detail and rigorous quality control to ensure it meets our high standards.

Innovation

We continuously explore new designs, materials, and manufacturing techniques to bring fresh and exciting products to our customers.

Community

We believe in giving back to the community through fair wages and supporting local artisans and crafters.
